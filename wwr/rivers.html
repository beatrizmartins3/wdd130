RIVERS

The 5 best rivers in the world for rafting.

1- Alsek and Tatshenshini Rivers, Alaska and Canada

Glaciers and icebergs are among the attractions of the Alsek and Tatshenshini rivers, which flow through the magnificent and remote landscapes of tundra and mountains on the Canadian-Alaska border.

2- Middle Fork, United States

Rapids that reach level IV and landscapes of forests and mountains with a lot of wildlife are the attractions for rafting in the Middle Fork.

3- Upano River, Ecuador

Surrounded by Amazonian vegetation, the river has rapids from level III to IV, with canyons and waterfalls along the way.

4- Johnstone River, Australia

Grade IV and V rapids amid volcanoes and rainforests in Palmerston National Park await adventurers on the Johnstone River in northeastern Australia.

5- Sun Kosi River, Nepal

In the Nepal region, level V rapids take adrenaline lovers to venture at high speed between canyons and gorges, to reach a stretch surrounded by dense tropical vegetation.
